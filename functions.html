Function Reference

  • bleed(text)

    Outputs text that drips down the screen.

  • whisper(message)

    Fades in and out like something behind you.

  • mirror(self)

    Shows your reflection in the code.

  • corrupt(data)

    Slowly decays displayed text.

  • watch(user)

    Tracks behavior. Adjusts display accordingly.
